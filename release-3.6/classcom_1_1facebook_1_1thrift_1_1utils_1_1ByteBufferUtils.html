static byte [] com.facebook.thrift.utils.ByteBufferUtils.toBytes ( ByteBuffer  buffer)
static

Convert the ByteBuffer content into a byte[] without consuming the ByteBuffer (i.e.

without moving the position).

If the ByteBuffer is backed by a byte[] and the position and limit are at the limit of the buffer (resp. 0 and capacity), the underlying byte[] is returned without any copy.
